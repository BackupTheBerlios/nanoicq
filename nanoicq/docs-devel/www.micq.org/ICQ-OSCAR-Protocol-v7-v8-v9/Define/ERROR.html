#define ERROR

The error codes used in SNAC (*, 1).

ERROR_HEADER 1 Invalid SNAC header.
ERROR_RATE_SERVER 2 Server rate limit exceeded.
ERROR_RATE_CLIENT 3 Client rate limit exceeded.
ERROR_OFFLINE 4 Recipient is offline.
ERROR_SERVICE_NA 5 Requested service is not available.
ERROR_SERVICE_UNDEF 6 Requested service is undefined.
ERROR_OBSOLETE 7 The sent SNAC is obsolete.
ERROR_UNSUP_SERVER 8 Not suppoerted by server.
ERROR_UNSUP_CLIENT 9 Not suppoerted by client.
ERROR_REFUSED_CLIENT 10 (0x0a) Refused by client.
ERROR_REPLY_BIG 11 (0x0b) The reply was too big.
ERROR_RESPONSE_LOST 12 (0x0c) Responses lost.
ERROR_REQ_DENIED 13 (0x0d) The request was denied.
ERROR_SNAC 14 (0x0e) Invalid SNAC.
ERROR_RIGHTS 15 (0x0f) Insufficient rights.
ERROR_BLOCKED 16 (0x10) Recipient blocked (in local permit/deny).
ERROR_EVIL_SENDER 17 (0x11) The sender is too evil.
ERROR_EVIL_RECIP 18 (0x12) The recipient is too evil.
ERROR_RECIP_UNAVAIL 19 (0x13) User temporarily unavailable
ERROR_NO_MATCH 20 (0x14) No match found.
ERROR_LIST_OVER 21 (0x15) List overflow.
ERROR_REQ_AMBIG 22 (0x16) Ambiguous request.
ERROR_QUEUE_FULL 23 (0x17) Server queue full.
ERROR_IS_AOL 24 (0x18) Not on AOL.
